Replace the Function App modules' individual built-in storage RBAC assignments with assignments to merged custom roles for host storage and durable storage.

## Migration Guide

When you apply the upgrade, Terraform will replace the existing built-in storage role assignments with assignments to merged custom roles. In practice this change is expected to produce more deletes than creates while the RBAC model is consolidated.

The main operational risk is limited to the apply window itself: Function Apps may temporarily fail to access their host or durable storage while the old assignments are being removed and the new merged assignments are being created.
