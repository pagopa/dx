DX Plugin for AIEPFD extends Copilot with product development skills for turning
ideas into PRDs, design reviews, use cases, and Jira-ready story drafts.

## Plugin resources

- [Plugin manifest](https://github.com/pagopa/dx/blob/main/plugins/aiepfd/.plugin/plugin.json)

## Available skills

- [write-prd](https://github.com/pagopa/dx/blob/main/plugins/aiepfd/skills/write-prd/SKILL.md)
  — Draft product requirement documents from product goals and repository
  context
- [write-design-review](https://github.com/pagopa/dx/blob/main/plugins/aiepfd/skills/write-design-review/SKILL.md)
  — Prepare engineering design reviews with architecture, alternatives, and
  risks
- [write-use-cases](https://github.com/pagopa/dx/blob/main/plugins/aiepfd/skills/write-use-cases/SKILL.md)
  — Convert feature intent into structured use cases with actors and flows
- [use-cases-to-jira-stories](https://github.com/pagopa/dx/blob/main/plugins/aiepfd/skills/use-cases-to-jira-stories/SKILL.md)
  — Decompose validated use cases into Jira-ready story drafts
